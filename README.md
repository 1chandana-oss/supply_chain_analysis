# 🚚 Supply Chain Analytics | End-to-End Business Intelligence Project

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Supply Chain Analytics** solution developed using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**. The objective is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and logistics efficiency to generate actionable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based business analysis, and interactive Power BI dashboard development.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Supply chain organizations generate large volumes of operational data. However, identifying revenue drivers, improving delivery performance, and optimizing inventory remain significant challenges.

This project aims to answer key business questions such as:

- Which markets generate the highest revenue and profit?
- Which customer segments contribute the most to business growth?
- Which products should be prioritized for inventory planning?
- How effective are different shipping modes?
- What factors contribute to late deliveries?
- What strategic actions can improve supply chain performance?

# 📈 Key Business Insights

- Generated **$36.78M** in total revenue.
- Achieved **$3.97M** in total profit.
- Processed **65,752 orders** from **20,652 customers**.
- Europe and LATAM generated the highest revenue.
- Consumer customers contributed the largest share of sales.
- Standard Class handled the highest shipment volume.
- Late delivery risk was approximately **54.8%**.
- A small percentage of products generated the majority of revenue (ABC Principle).

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve shipping operations to reduce delivery delays.
- Prioritize inventory for high-performing products.
- Strengthen customer retention strategies.
- Review low-margin products for pricing optimization.
- Monitor operational KPIs using interactive dashboards.
